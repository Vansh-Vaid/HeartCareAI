Audit notes
  • Legacy derived columns are dropped unless they are explicitly validated for the live feature contract.
  • Clinical-range sanitization is applied consistently in training and inference.
  • Outlier rows are not removed globally, so extreme but clinically plausible cases are preserved.
  • Probability calibration is part of the promoted artifact, not a UI-only display choice.
