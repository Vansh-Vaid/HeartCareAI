About HeartCare AI

A simpler way to understand heart screening results

HeartCare AI is designed to help patients and clinicians review heart screening information more clearly, with guided forms, simple explanations, and downloadable reports.

What this app helps you do
  • Enter heart-related test values in one guided screening form.
  • Receive a risk estimate with explanation notes and easy-to-read labels.
  • Save reports for future review or share them with a doctor.
  • Use the built-in guide to understand difficult terms in plain language.
  • Keep the result in context: the app supports follow-up, not diagnosis.
How the app stays careful
Checks inputs
Looks for missing or invalid values before showing a result.
Explains results
Shows the estimate, confidence wording, and likely reasons behind it.
Saves reports
Lets users download a summary for later review.
Adds guidance
The help guide explains terms and reminds users to consult a clinician.
