DATA CLEANING & WHO MEDICAL GUIDELINES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Medical safety limits applied during data cleaning:

  1. AGE: 18-100 years
     Age in years (adult)

  2. RESTING BLOOD PRESSURE (mmHg): 80-200
     Resting BP: Normal <120, Elevated 120-129, High Stage 1 130-139, Stage 2 >=140

  3. SERUM CHOLESTEROL (mg/dL): 100-400
     Cholesterol: Desirable <200, Borderline 200-239, High >=240

  4. MAX HEART RATE ACHIEVED (bpm): 60-220
     Max heart rate: Healthy 60-100 bpm resting, target 60-100% of max with exercise

  5. ST DEPRESSION: 0-10
     ST depression induced by exercise (normal: 0, higher indicates more abnormality)
